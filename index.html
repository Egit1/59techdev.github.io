Our Bible-centered Values

What We
Believe


Salvation


We believe in salvatin by God's grace, through faith in what Jesus Christ accomplished on the cross.Salvation is available to all who will truly believe in God's redemption plan, which is Jesus Christ, the way and the truth, and the life


The Holy Spirit


We believe in God the Holy Spirit. He regenerates and indwells the believer, baptizing them into the Body of Christ and empowering them to live victorious lives. The Holy Spirit is the teacher, the guide, and the One who empowers the Christian



The Scriptures


We believe that the Bible, both Old Testament and New Testament, is the divinely authoritative Word of God and the standard for every age and for every life. We believe it is without error and its authority is final and eternal.
